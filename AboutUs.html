OUR MISSION

To engage in commercial avocado production while

contributing to environmental conservation, creating employment,

offering training and technology transfer to the local community.





CORE VALUES

  • Highest standards of Integrity
  • Respect for individuals
  • Dedication to helping our workers and customers
  • Innovations
  • Teamwork








OUR VISION

To supply avocados in a large scale within the country and abroad.





CREDENTIALS

The farm has in the past attracted the attention of both the government and non-governmental organizations. In year 2009, it received the trophy for the best small-scale farmer in Meru from Agricultural Society of Kenya and Best Farmer, Ndurumo ya Arithi FDA from Mount Kenya East Pilot Project for Natural Resource Management (MKEPP). The farm has a certificate in recognition of its contribution on food security by the United Nations’ FAO and the Kenya’s Minister of Agriculture.
